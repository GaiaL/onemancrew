Can a Lightbulb in the Mouth be Taken Out?

Video (2011)

“You can’t put a light bulb in your mouth” is an old urban myth. Copying Mythbusters won’t do, our group designed, directed and shoot a short piece, showing a series of experiments and all the precautions we did.
Our actor – who is a fellow editor - is known as the “bulb man” on Chinese internet ever since then.
